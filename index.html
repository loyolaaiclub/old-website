Loyola AI Club

EST 2021

    Like the industrial revolution, artificial intelligence is changing life as we know it. AI may eventually become fundamental to everyday work, just like reading and writing. We meet to exchange ideas related to interdisciplinary AI use and apply them in real world situations in order to give students from all walks of life exposure to tools that will enhance the way they contribute to society.
    We bring guest speakers to campus to discuss how they use AI in the real world and host workshops to help students build technical skills. We intend to build a network of students and alumni and foster professional development.
